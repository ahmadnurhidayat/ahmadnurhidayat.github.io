A practical, 4-lab series on deploying and managing Active Directory infrastructure. From initial Domain Controller setup through advanced Group Policy and multi-site replication, these labs cover the identity foundation that powers enterprise Windows environments.

## Lab Progression

**Lab 1 — Active Directory Domain Services & DNS Setup:**
Deploy your first Domain Controller, configure DNS zones, and establish the domain. Learn the fundamentals of AD DS, forest and domain concepts, and how DNS is integral to AD operation.

**Lab 2 — Group Policy Fundamentals:**
Create and manage Group Policy Objects (GPOs) to enforce security settings, deploy software, and configure user environments. Understand GPO processing order, inheritance, and troubleshooting.

**Lab 3 — Advanced Group Policy & Software Deployment:**
Advanced GPO techniques — WMI filtering, Group Policy Preferences, loopback processing, and deploying MSI packages. Build sophisticated policy configurations that scale across OUs.

**Lab 4 — Multi-DC Architecture & Replication:**
Deploy a second Domain Controller, configure sites and subnets, manage replication topology, and implement site-aware services. Understand how AD ensures consistency across distributed deployments.

## Technologies

Active Directory Domain Services, DNS Server, Group Policy Management, Windows Server, PowerShell, SMB/CIFS
